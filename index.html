Doppelgang or FindYourDouble The idea behind this app is that you upload a photograph of yourself and get the closest match back. The photo can be of your face or full body. Later you will be able to submit short personality and intelligence tests to find other people who have similar characteristics. There will be serious matches and an option for a pot luck match.

Many people have a fascination with themselves. Are they unique? Wouldn't it be amazing if there was someone who looked just like me.? Doesn't anyone else think like me? These types of questions can be answered at FindYourDouble.

FAQ

What is Dietorium?
Dietorium is what we like to think of as "A place for dieters". Really what we are doing is providing a whole set of tools that are integrated together as a suite that allow users to track their diets, exercise programs, and objectively measure their results.

The really cool part is that you will be a part of a community, where you can share diet or meal plans, exercise routines, and even work together with a personal trainer if you choose.

One of the prospects that we are really excited about is the idea of being able to provide summary data to our users such as current diet and exercise trends, even what diets are working best for different types of goals, etc.
What is our motivation?
We are scratching our own itch so to speak. This is what excites us, and these are the tools we wish we already had.
What about Coaches & Personal Trainers?
We know how important a good personal trainer or coach can be.

Our dream is to help facilitate the ability for coaches and trainers to train individuals across the internet. Not only will you be able to reach more individuals but you will be able to easily manage a larger number of individuals at once.

One of the primary features of Dietorium will be the ability for Coaches & Personal Trainers to be able to collaborate with their athletes / clients. Our vision includes the ability for the sharing of documents, diets, exercise routines, and messages with your clients. Imagine having the ability to quickly setup an exercise and diet program for a client based off a template you designed.

What we believe is the killer feature, is the ability for you to collect payment directly from your clients through the site.
